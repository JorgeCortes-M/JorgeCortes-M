## About Me

I am a Molecular Biotechnology Engineer and evolutionary biologist with a Ph.D. in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (University of Chile, 2025). My research sits at the intersection of population genomics, ecotoxicology, and invasion biology, with a particular focus on how natural populations respond — genetically and epigenetically — to environmental stressors such as chronic pollution and biological invasions.

I am passionate about teaching, scientific communication, and the integration of computational approaches into biological research. This GitHub reflects that intersection: a space where ecology meets data science.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Population genomics & microevolution** — signatures of selection, genetic drift, and local adaptation
- **Ecotoxicogenomics** — genomic and transcriptomic responses to chronic pollution in freshwater fish
- **Biological invasions** — evolutionary dynamics of invasive species across Mediterranean river systems
- **Bioinformatics & statistical genomics** — ABC methods, demographic inference, genome scans
